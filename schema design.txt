Step 1 – Schema Design (Normalization up to 3NF)
We design four tables: Students, Courses, Instructors, Enrollments.
To be in 3NF:
Each table has a primary key
No repeating groups
No partial dependency
No transitive dependency
Enrollment table resolves the many-to-many relationship between Students and Courses


Relational Schema

Students
student_id (PK)
name
email (UNIQUE)
age (CHECK age > 17)

Instructors
instructor_id (PK)
name
department

Courses
course_id (PK)
title
credits
instructor_id (FK)

Enrollments
student_id (FK)
course_id (FK)
grade
Primary Key (student_id, course_id)
